SpreadSwipe Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 28, 2026

Overview

SpreadSwipe is a watchOS app that displays sports point spread information. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect — which is essentially nothing.

Data Collection

SpreadSwipe does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account required — You do not need to create an account or sign in.
  • No personal data collected — We do not collect your name, email, location, or any other personal information.
  • No analytics or tracking — We do not use any analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.
  • No data shared with third parties — We do not share any data with third parties.

Locally Stored Data

The app stores the following data only on your device using Apple's standard on-device storage:

  • Team preferences — Your selected favorite teams (up to 5).
  • Cached spreads — The most recently fetched point spread data, so the app can display information even when offline.

This data never leaves your device and is automatically removed if you delete the app.

Network Requests

The app makes network requests to our server to fetch current point spread data. These requests do not contain any personally identifiable information. Only the request itself (a standard HTTPS GET) is made — no user data, device identifiers, or tokens are sent.

Children's Privacy

SpreadSwipe does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. The app does not facilitate gambling — it displays publicly available sports information only.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
