SpreadSwipe Support

About

SpreadSwipe is a watchOS app for quickly checking NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, and MLS point spreads right from your wrist. Swipe through your favorite teams to see current lines, upcoming game times, and opponents.

How It Works

  • Add teams — Long-press the screen to open Settings, then tap "Add Team" to select up to 5 favorite teams.
  • View spreads — Swipe vertically to browse your teams. Each page shows the spread, opponent, game date/time, and whether it's a home or away game.
  • Remove teams — In Settings, swipe left on a team to delete it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why don't I see a spread?

If the team's league is in the off-season or there are no upcoming games with published lines, you'll see a dash (—) instead of a spread.

How often are spreads updated?

Spread data is refreshed every 8 hours from our server, plus periodically via watchOS background refresh.

Is this a gambling app?

No. SpreadSwipe is an informational app that displays publicly available point spread data. It does not facilitate, enable, or encourage placing bets.
